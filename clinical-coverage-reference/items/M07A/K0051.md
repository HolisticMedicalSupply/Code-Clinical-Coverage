# K0051 - Cam Release Assembly (Replacement)

## Clinical Overview

K0051 represents the cam release assembly for power wheelchair footrest or legrest for **REPLACEMENT ONLY**. This is the quick-release mechanism (lever or cam) that allows footrest to swing away or detach for transfers and caregiver access. Cam release assemblies are BUNDLED at initial wheelchair issue with power wheelchairs and power tilt/recline and **cannot be billed separately at initial delivery**. K0051 is only billable as replacement when original cam release becomes broken or worn.

**Key Features:**
- Quick-release mechanism for footrest/legrest
- Allows footrest to swing away or detach
- Essential for transfers requiring footrest removal
- REPLACEMENT ONLY - BUNDLED at initial wheelchair issue

**REPLACEMENT ONLY:** Cam release assembly is BUNDLED at initial with power wheelchairs and power tilt/recline. K0051 cannot be billed when delivering new wheelchair - only as replacement when damaged.

## Coverage Details

### Medicare Coverage Criteria

**Covered:** Yes (REPLACEMENT ONLY)

**Requirements:**
- **Standard Written Order (SWO)** required
- **Medical necessity for swingaway footrest capability**
- **Documentation of transfer needs** requiring footrest removal:
  - Patient performs side transfers
  - Slide board transfers requiring access
  - Caregiver-assisted transfers needing footrest removed
  - Stand-pivot transfers where footrest interferes

**For Replacement:**
- **Documentation of cam release mechanism damage** (broken, worn, non-functional)
- **Documentation that cam release cannot be repaired**
- **Patient continues to require swingaway capability** for transfers

**Prior Authorization:** NOT required

**Face-to-Face:** Required ONLY with new power wheelchair, NOT for replacement alone *(Under Review - High Confidence)*

**Frequency:** Reasonable replacement as needed *(Under Review - High Confidence)*

**Quantity:** As needed per damaged cam release (typically 1 or 2 depending on how many footrests have damaged cam release) *(Under Review - High Confidence)*

**Important Notes:**
- REPLACEMENT ONLY - BUNDLED at initial with power wheelchairs, power tilt/recline
- Cannot bill at initial wheelchair delivery - included in base wheelchair
- KX modifier required for medical necessity documentation
- PT/OT evaluation not required for replacement
- RESNA ATP not required for replacement
- Face-to-face only if cam release part of NEW wheelchair order

## Cam Release Assembly Function

### What It Does:
- **Quick-release mechanism:** Lever or cam that releases footrest from wheelchair frame
- **Swingaway capability:** Allows footrest to swing to side or completely detach
- **Transfer access:** Provides clear access for patient/caregiver during transfers
- **Quick operation:** One-handed release for rapid footrest removal

### Why Swingaway Matters:
- **Side transfers:** Patient can transfer laterally with footrest swung away
- **Slide board transfers:** Provides access for slide board placement
- **Caregiver access:** Allows caregiver to approach patient for assisted transfers
- **Stand-pivot transfers:** Removes footrest obstruction when patient stands
- **Safety:** Clear access reduces transfer-related falls and injuries

## Transfer Types Requiring Swingaway

### Patient-Performed Transfers:
- **Side transfers:** Sliding laterally from wheelchair to bed/chair/car
- **Slide board transfers:** Using transfer board requiring clear side access
- **Stand-pivot transfers:** Where footrest position interferes with standing

### Caregiver-Assisted Transfers:
- **Dependent transfers:** Caregiver lifting/assisting patient
- **Two-person transfers:** Multiple caregivers needing access
- **Mechanical lift transfers:** Sling placement requiring footrest removal
- **Positioning access:** Caregiver needs to approach from front/side

## Common Denial Reasons

1. **"Billed at initial wheelchair delivery"** - Attempting to bill K0051 with new wheelchair. Fix: Do NOT bill K0051 at initial wheelchair issue - cam release assembly is BUNDLED with power wheelchairs and power tilt/recline. Only bill K0051 as replacement on existing wheelchair.

2. **"Medical necessity not established"** - Insufficient justification for swingaway footrest. Fix: Document specific transfer needs: "Patient performs independent side transfers from wheelchair to bed using slide board. Requires footrest swingaway capability to create clear side access for slide board placement. Cannot perform transfers safely with fixed footrest - creates obstruction and fall risk."

3. **"Patient does not perform transfers"** - Patient fully dependent, transfers by mechanical lift. Fix: Explain caregiver/mechanical access needs: "Patient transferred by mechanical lift. Caregiver requires footrest swingaway capability to access patient for sling placement. Cam release allows caregivers to swing footrest away for safe sling positioning and lift operation."

4. **"Insufficient documentation of damage"** (for replacement). Fix: Provide specific details: "Right footrest cam release mechanism broken. Lever will not release footrest. Mechanism jammed - footrest cannot swing away. Cannot perform transfers safely without functional cam release. Mechanism cannot be repaired - internal cam damaged. Replacement necessary."

5. **"Missing KX modifier"** - Billed without required modifier. Fix: KX modifier required to indicate medical necessity requirements met for swingaway footrest capability.

## Tips for Approval

**Medicare:**
- Document **specific transfer type** patient performs or caregiver performs
- Explain **why footrest removal necessary** for safe transfers
- For replacement: Document **cam release mechanism failure** and how it impacts transfers
- Note patient **continues to require swingaway capability** for transfers
- Include **KX modifier** indicating criteria met
- SWO required before dispensing

**NY Medicaid:**
- **Obtain prior authorization** for power wheelchair accessory before dispensing
- Same detailed documentation as Medicare
- Fiscal order from physician required
- Document medical necessity for transfer access

**Clinical Documentation Best Practices:**
- **Transfer needs:** "Patient performs [independent side transfers/slide board transfers/stand-pivot transfers/caregiver-assisted transfers]. Requires footrest swingaway capability to [create clear side access/allow slide board placement/remove obstruction when standing/provide caregiver access]. Cam release mechanism allows quick footrest removal for safe transfer performance."
- **Damage description (replacement):** "Cam release assembly broken/worn on [left/right/both] footrest. Mechanism will not release footrest. [Lever jammed/cam damaged/release mechanism failed]. Cannot swing footrest away for transfers. Patient unable to perform [transfer type] safely without functional cam release. Mechanism cannot be repaired - internal damage. Replacement necessary for safe transfer capability."
- **Safety justification:** "Functional cam release essential for safe transfers. Broken cam release prevents footrest removal, creating transfer obstruction and fall risk. Patient requires reliable swingaway mechanism for [independent/assisted] transfer safety."
